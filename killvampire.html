Vampire Pixel Survivors

Survive waves of pixel monsters in this addictive horror-themed survival game!

About The Game

Vampire Pixel Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you control a vampire hunter trying to survive increasingly difficult waves of pixel monsters. Use strategy and quick reflexes to defeat enemies, collect power-ups, and survive as long as possible!

How To Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Attacks are automatic - just position yourself strategically to defeat waves of enemies. Collect gems to level up and unlock powerful new abilities.

Retro Pixel Graphics

Enjoy nostalgic pixel art visuals that combine modern gameplay with classic gaming aesthetics.

Challenging Gameplay

Test your skills against increasingly difficult waves of enemies that will push your survival abilities to the limit.

Unique Abilities

Unlock powerful vampire-hunting weapons and abilities as you progress through the game.

Game Tips

Focus on movement and positioning rather than attacking. Prioritize leveling up your favorite weapons early. Don't get cornered by large groups of enemies!
